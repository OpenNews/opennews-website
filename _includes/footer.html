OpenNews connects a network of developers, designers, journalists and editors to collaborate on open technologies and transform processes within journalism. OpenNews believes that a community of peers working, learning and solving problems together can create a stronger, more responsive, anti-racist, andequitable journalism ecosystem.

Incubated at Mozilla from 2011-2016, OpenNews is now a project of Community Partners.
