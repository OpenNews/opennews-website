OpenNews receives Press Forward funding to develop emergency preparedness infrastructure for news

(Photo by John Hernandez)

OpenNews is one of 22 organizations sharing $22.7 million to meet the urgent challenges local newsrooms face.

OpenNews, NC Local, and Newspack will create “Emergency Mode for News” to equip local newsrooms with a plan and support to keep their communities informed when disasters happen with funding from Press Forward, a national initiative to reimagine local news.
